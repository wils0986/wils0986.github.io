About Me

My name is Jenny, I am 27 years old and I am origianlly from Toronto, ON. I recently came to the conclusion that my career as a Hairstylist was coming to an end. I have been in the industry for about eight years which had definite highs and lows. I felt that such a physically demanding career may not be a great choice as I get older, as well as working nights, weekends, not having the security of a salary, and finally being out of work for many months due to Covid-19 lockdowns was not sustainable. So, I started to consider a career that is more relevant to my interests, as well as one that could carry on virtually if need be. I have always had a passion for PCs, both for gaming as well as learning as much as possible about how to interact with software and hardware. My father practiced coding for his entire career, which for him started back in the 60's and consisted of programming things like ATMs and flight simulators. He went on to spend the majority of his adult career working for RBC. As a result, I had my own PC (with internet) in my bedroom by age two, which I believe strongly contributed to my interest in computers and the world of technology.

For my 2nd paragraph, I thought I would talk about music. I have loved rock, punk, metal, and most genres considered alternative since I can remember. One of my all time favorite bands who you may know, you seem pretty cool, is The Used. I saw them in concert a few years back which was a dream come true! I also really enjoy classic rock, like the Hip, who would have been incredible to see live. I have been dying to see a concert for what feels like eternity.

It ain't what you don't know that gets you into trouble. It's what you know for sure that just ain't so. MARK TWAIN
